About

The spring school especially focuses on PhD students, post-doctoral researchers and senior researchers from Universities, research institutes, who have a clear interest in learning the basics as well as the latest developments in computer aided drug design. The program provides an overview of the main approaches and techniques used in computational drug design. During the afternoon session, the participants will have the opportunity to obtain hands-on training via computational exercises.
